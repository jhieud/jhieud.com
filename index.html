About

Hi! I'm Hieu, a second-year student at Northeastern University majoring in Computer Science with minors in Game Art and Interaction Design. I am a software engineer, artist, designer, and game developer.

Much of my design work has been for cultural organizations like the Vietnamese-American Community of Massachusetts and the New England Intercollegiate Vietnamese Student Association. Through my experiences with them, I developed a passion for community and advocacy.

Outside of class, I am a member of both the Northeastern Game Development Club and NU Knits, a club at Northeastern dedicated to fiber arts.

Coding Projects

D&D 5e Character Generator

Github

My final project for Programming Basics is an open-ended website of my choosing. Since I like playing D&D with my friends but despise the character creation process, I decided to make this.

The D&D 5e Character Generator is a tool for making first-level D&D characters. Users can either fill the fields manually or press a button to make a completely random character. All information is based on the 5e Player's handbook.

Lion Dance Simulator

Github

My final project for 5D Art Fundamentals is an interactive experience made using Twine. This is a short choose-your-own-adventure game based on my own experiences as a lion dancer.

Play as a member of a lion dance team at a Lunar New Year festival. Do you have what it takes to satisfy the crowd?
